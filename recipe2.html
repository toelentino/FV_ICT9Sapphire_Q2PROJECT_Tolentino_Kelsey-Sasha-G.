Thai Chicken Pandan

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Chicken wings 6 pieces
Fresh Pandan leaves 6 to 8 pieces
Soy Sauce 2 teaspoons
Fish Sauce 2 tablespoons
Sugar 1/2 teaspoon
Ground black pepper 1/2 teaspoon
Garlic, minced 2-3 cloves
Calamansi Juice 1 tablespoon
Cooking oil 2 cups

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, fish sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, black pepper, garlic, ginger, and lemon juice.
  2. Add chicken pieces to the mixture and marinate for at least 3 hours, preferably overnight in the fridge to enhance flavor.
  3. Wrap the chicken in Pandan leaves by covering it with the leaf and tying it securely.
  4. Heat oil in a pot and deep fry the chicken until golden brown, or grill it instead.
  5. Turn off the heat and transfer the chicken to a serving plate.
  6. Enjoy your dish!
