Gaeng Keow Waan

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Boneless skinless chicken 3 lbs
Coconut milk 4 cups
Fish sauce 2 tablespoons
Laos powder 3 pcs
Green curry paste 3 teaspoons
Fresh sweet basil leaves 1/2 cup
Green peas 1/2 cup
Serrano chilies 7 pcs
Eggplant 1/2 cup

Instructions

  1. In a wok or large frying pan, combine chicken, 2 cups coconut milk, fish sauce, and laos powder. Bring to a boil and cook until the chicken is tender.
  2. Remove the chicken with a slotted spoon, leaving the liquid behind, and set it aside.
  3. Let the milk continue boiling until it thickens and becomes oily.
  4. Add curry paste to the pan and cook for 3 minutes until smooth.
  5. Return the chicken to the pan, add the remaining coconut milk, and bring the mixture back to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat and simmer for about 10 minutes.
  7. Add basil, citrus leaves, peas, serranos, and eggplant, then raise the heat and boil for 5 more minutes.
  8. Enjoy!
