Gai Yang

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Chicken thigh fillets (skinless, boneless) 1 kg
Lemongrass stalk 1 pc
Garlic 6 cloves
Lime juice 2 tablespoons
Chopped red chili 2 teaspoons
Fish sauce 3 tablespoons
Palm sugar 3 tablespoons
Honey 2 tablespoons
Black pepper 1/2 tablespoon

Instructions

  1. Place marinade ingredients in a large ziplock bag and massage to mix.
  2. Add chicken to the bag, massaging to coat evenly. Marinate for at least 3 hours, preferably overnight (up to 24 hours).
  3. Remove chicken from the marinade and discard the marinade.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high or heat 1/2 tbsp oil in a non-stick pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Cook the chicken for about 3 minutes per side until golden brown.
  6. Rest the chicken for a few minutes, then serve with lime wedges and garnish with fresh chilies and cilantro, if desired.
